Our Approach

At SELS, the goal is not only to improve English ability, but to help children become comfortable using English in everyday life.

We combine language learning with a wide range of activities, allowing children to naturally connect English with action, thinking, and creativity.

Children are encouraged to try, make mistakes, communicate, and express their ideas. This builds confidence and independence.

What We Do

English Through Activities

Children use English while playing games, doing group activities, and interacting with others.

Programming & Technology

Programming is introduced in English, helping children develop logic and communication at the same time

Science & Experiments

Hands-on experiments help children explore curiosity while using English to explain and think.

Cooking & Culture

Children experience different cultures and ideas through cooking and activities in English.

Creative Projects

Art and creative work allow children to express themselves and communicate ideas in English.

Our Goal

The goal of SELS is not to force advanced knowledge early, but to help children become comfortable using English while exploring different fields.

By combining English with science, technology, art, and culture, children develop both language skills and curiosity about the world.

This helps them discover what they enjoy and what they may want to pursue in the future.
