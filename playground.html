Playground

Quick challenge games designed to test different skills in a fun and interactive way.

What is the Playground?

Playground is a space for small interactive games that can quickly test different abilities. These are not long or complex games. The idea is to try something simple, react quickly, and see how you do.

Some games focus on speed, some on memory, some on attention, and some on unusual ways of thinking.

What Can You Test?

The Playground is designed around different types of quick challenges, including reaction, memory, reflex, luck, visual attention, listening, and non-standard thinking.
